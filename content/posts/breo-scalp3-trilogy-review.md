## Red Light Therapy: The Science Behind the Benefits

Red light therapy has gained significant attention in recent years for its remarkable benefits to scalp and hair health:

1. **Improved Blood Circulation**: The 850nm red light penetrates the scalp, increasing blood flow to hair follicles, which delivers essential nutrients and oxygen.

2. **Hair Follicle Activation**: Red light stimulates the production of ATP (adenosine triphosphate) in hair follicles, promoting cellular energy and encouraging hair growth.

3. **Reduced Inflammation**: The therapy helps calm scalp inflammation, addressing issues like dandruff and itchiness.

4. **Strengthened Hair Follicles**: Regular use can help strengthen hair follicles, reducing hair loss and improving hair thickness.

5. **Enhanced Product Absorption**: When used with hair care products, red light therapy improves their penetration and effectiveness.
